Our Mission

Beautiful Books, Made Accessible

At Ciborium Press, our goal is to curate an affordable and accessible library of Christian classics, bound in beauty, that allows the wisdom of the faith to persevere.

Available through Amazon and major retailers, our collections bring together careful typesetting, elegant cover art, and accessible pricing.
